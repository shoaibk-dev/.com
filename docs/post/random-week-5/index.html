Random article

GetPocket is a service where you can save good articles within 1-click extension. And it has an Explore page, where you can find good article recommended by people. This is what I got from it, Stop Spending So Much Time In Your Head

The main idea is :

99% of your thoughts are useless !

So stop thinking and start feeling.

Then I start :

Random bible
